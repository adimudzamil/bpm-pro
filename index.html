HOW TO USE

  1. Access BPM: Log into BPM
  2. Navigate to Passenger List: Go to your flight → Click "Passenger List"
  3. Select All Data:
    • On android mobile, must use xclipper available on play store due to android copy limit restrictions: Long press on the flight number(FLT NBR), select all, then use xcopy from xclipper or drag to select all passenger data down to "Showing X entries"
    • On iOS: Select from FLT NBR until end of pax list > copy
    • On desktop: Click before flight number (FLT NBR), hold Shift, scroll down and click after "Showing X entries"
  4. Copy: Tap/Click "Copy" from the menu
  5. Paste Here: Tap/Click in the text box above and paste (Ctrl+V or long press → Paste)
  6. Generate: Click "Generate Report" button (or press Ctrl+Enter). Simplest form for everyone
  7. Advanced Settings (NEW!): Click "Advanced Settings" to select your aircraft type for accurate zone breakdown. Apply zone filters to get the detailed breakdown or use custom configurations to create a custom list for working positions.
  8. Print: Use "Print Report Only" button for paper-saving print (or press Ctrl+P)

Aircraft Configuration Guide:

  • 737NG/MAX: Business rows 1-3, Economy rows 4-31
  • 737NR: Business rows 1-4, Economy rows 5-30
  • A330-300/900: Business 1-8, Zone C 9-27, Zone D 28-44
  • A330-200: Business 1-6, Zone C 14-30, Zone D 31-50
  • A350-900: Business 1-11, Zone C 12-22, Zone D 23-32, Zone E 33-41
  • A350(MAH): Business 1-10, Zone C 20-35, Zone D 36-46, Zone E 47-55
  • Custom: Define your own zones for any aircraft type
